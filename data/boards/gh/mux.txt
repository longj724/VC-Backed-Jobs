  <h1>Mux</h1>

  <div id="content">
    <p><a class="external" href="https://mux.com">Mux</a>&nbsp;is building the future of online video infrastructure.</p>
<p>Our first product was analytics and performance monitoring - think “NewRelic for Video” - used by major video streaming companies like Vimeo, PBS, and the New York Times to monitor and improve billions of streams.</p>
<p>In early 2018, we launched&nbsp;<a class="external" href="https://mux.com/video">Mux Video</a>, a simple API to video hosting, encoding and streaming - think "Stripe for Video" - which uses our performance data, machine learning, and just-in-time encoding for unmatched video quality and efficiency. POST a video in; GET a video out that plays anywhere and is deeply optimized for the target device and user. Then get creative with features like audio transcription and face detection. We think building with video should be fun, not complicated.</p>
<p>You’ll be joining an&nbsp;<a class="external" href="https://mux.com/team">amazing team</a>&nbsp;from places like Google/YouTube, Facebook, Twitch and Brightcove. Our founders previously started (and sold)&nbsp;Zencoder, an early leader in cloud video technology. We also authored&nbsp;Video.js, the biggest HTML5 video player on the web. We were named one of the&nbsp;<a class="external" href="https://techcrunch.com/gallery/the-top-8-startups-from-y-combinator-winter-16-demo-day-2/">best Y Combinator startups of 2016</a>, and we recently raised a&nbsp;Series A&nbsp;from Accel, joining existing investors SV Angel, Lowercase, the founders of Heroku, Parse, and others.</p>
