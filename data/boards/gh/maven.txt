  <h1>Maven</h1>

  <div id="content">
    <p>Maven is building the university of the future - empowering the world’s creators to offer cohort-based courses directly to their audiences. Courses on Maven have community at their core, focus on active learning, and are available anywhere in the world.</p>
<p>Our team has raised $25 million from incredible investors including Andreessen Horowitz, First Round, Naval, and Arlan Hamilton, and we've launched sold-out courses with instructors like <a href="https://maven.com/li/creator-economy-course">Li Jin</a>, <a href="https://www.pompscryptocourse.com/" target="_blank">Anthony Pompliano,</a> <a href="http://maven.com/lenny/product-management-fundamentals">Lenny Rachitsky</a>, and <a href="https://www.minimalistentrepreneur.com/course">Sahil Lavingia</a>.</p>
<p>We're the leading player in a massive and growing market, but are still early enough for you to make a big impact. Join us!</p>
