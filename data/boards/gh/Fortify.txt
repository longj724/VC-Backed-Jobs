The company was founded on research on composite 3D printing by Randall Erb and Joshua Martin at Northeastern University. Their goal was to enable quick and seamless fabrication of composites with optimized microstructures. Through their research, they invented magnetic 3D printing, or <em>Fluxprint</em>. It’s a patented magnetic 3D printing process that creates optimized composites.
