  <h1>Gobble</h1>

  <div id="content">
    <p>Gobble is a Singapore headquartered group-buying food app for Asia Pacific. We’re out to conquer all university campuses across the region with a fun and discounted food ordering app based on your friend’s recommendations, and we’ve got top-tier VC backing us already!</p>
<p>Our team has led multiple ventures ranging from Deliveroo, Foodpanda, to the largest micromobility player in the world, Lime, and have backgrounds in top tier consulting firms like McKinsey &amp; Company. Our investors have led pioneering technology companies including Delivery Hero, Gojek, Uber, Lime, Tinder, amongst others.</p>
<p>We are like-minded in our desire to build the future of local commerce, starting with social checkout for restaurants. If you’re looking to work at a company that is mission-driven and is helping build the future for F&amp;B (and you love food), we'd love to work with you!</p>
