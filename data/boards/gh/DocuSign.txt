<div class="panel-separator"></div><div class="panel-pane pane-fieldable-panels-pane pane-current-3211 pane-bundle-content"  >

    <div class="field field-name-field-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><div class="flex-container">
	<div class="image"><img alt="Person sitting at desk working on computer" class="media-element file-default" data-delta="2" height="624" src="https://www.docusign.com/sites/default/files/careers-page-update_1.jpg" width="938" /></div>
	<div class="copy--right">
		<p>DocuSign offers better ways for teams and individuals to work by designating positions as In-Office, Flex or Remote. You can find the job designations listed on our job postings, and they are as follows:</p>

		<ul class="content-ul-list">
			<li><strong>Remote.</strong> You work in roles which allow you to work remotely 100% of the time.</li>
			<li><strong>Flex</strong>. You may choose to work 50% remotely. You also might be able to work more than 50% remotely, and you can discuss that with your manager. Which days will be remote and which will be in-office will be based on a combination of your needs, plus team and business needs as determined by your manager.</li>
			<li><strong>In-Office.</strong> These are positions that require you to be in the physical workspace, co-located with specific hardware systems, or are positions where minute-by-minute collaboration is important to deliver quality results.</li>
		</ul>

		<p>We’d encourage you to speak to your recruiter about these job designations and how we are evolving the way we work.</p>
